ABOUT

I am a Ph.D candidate in Prof. Xiaolei Huang's IDEA lab at Lehigh University. My research focuses on image analysis and computer vision for cell biology and biophysics, especially on segmentation and tracking of dynamic multidimensional curvilinear networks. I am the main designer and developer of the open source project SOAX.
